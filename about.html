Our Story

It was in the month of october 2018 and Cyclone titli had wrecked its havoc on the coast of Andhra and Orissa. A lot of units and small scale industries had suffered tremndous losses. One fine day we got a call from a small time business owner who had a small manufacturing unit in orissa. He explained us that his unit had suffered major losses and the machinery was highly damaged because of cyclone titlee. Because of lack of education, he did not know whom to contact for spare parts, eventually found our number through some source . We understood his requirement and satisfied his requirements.

This experience showed us what we could we do in a larger scale .There are millions of small scale units which either suffer because they dont have knowledge about their machinery or have tough time finding spares or are at the mercy of the local dealer and his steep prices.We are looking forward to break this barrier and connect the end user directly with the manufacturer and help the customer in getting genuine prices for original spare parts and be a one step solution for finding spares. Any one with any kind of machinery does not have to search or wander hopelessly around for spares for their machinery anymore.
